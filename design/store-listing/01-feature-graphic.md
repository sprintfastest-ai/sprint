# Feature Graphic — 1024 × 500 px

The Feature Graphic sits at the top of your Play Store listing and in
promotional banners. It must work with **no text overlay required by Play**
but should sell the app in one glance — most successful listings put the
logo + tagline + a hint of UI on it.

No device frame here — this is a wide banner, not a screenshot.

---

## Prompt A — Primary (recommended)

```
Create a Google Play Store Feature Graphic, exactly 1024x500 pixels,
landscape banner format, flat design (no drop shadows outside the described
elements, no photorealism).

Background: a bold diagonal gradient sweep from #1A6BB5 (deep blue) on the
left to #0F4A80 (darker blue) on the right, with a subtle diagonal speed-line
motif (3-4 thin angled streaks in #F05A1A orange and #6DC400 green, like
sprint-track lane markings, sweeping left to right at roughly 20 degrees,
fading to low opacity near the edges).

Left third: the SprintFastest runner icon — a stylised sprinting figure
rendered as a smooth gradient silhouette sweeping from blue through orange
to green, mid-stride, dynamic and forward-leaning — large and centered
vertically, roughly 380px tall.

Center-right: the wordmark "SprintFastest" in bold white sans-serif
(Inter/SF Pro Display Bold, roughly 72px), with the tagline "TRACK. TRAIN.
DOMINATE." beneath it in smaller orange (#F05A1A) uppercase letting, letter-
spaced, roughly 22px.

No phone mockups, no additional icons, no clutter — this is a wide banner
that must read clearly at thumbnail size. Leave 40px of breathing room on
all edges. Export as flat PNG, no transparency.
```

---

## Prompt B — UI-forward alternative

```
Create a Google Play Store Feature Graphic, exactly 1024x500 pixels,
landscape banner. Background is solid #F8F9FA light grey.

Right half: a single Android phone silhouette (simple rounded-rectangle
outline, no realistic bezel detail) tilted 8 degrees, displaying a cropped
view of a mobile app dashboard screen — a blue stat card showing a flame
icon and "12 Day Streak", a training session card below it with drill name
pills, all using colors #1A6BB5 blue, #F05A1A orange, #6DC400 green on white
cards with soft rounded corners (16px radius).

Left half: the SprintFastest runner icon (blue-orange-green gradient
sprinting figure) above the wordmark "SprintFastest" in bold #1A1A1A dark
text (Inter Bold, ~64px), with tagline "Track. Train. Dominate." in
#6B7280 grey beneath it.

Clean, modern, lots of white space, no photorealistic rendering — this is a
flat vector-style marketing graphic, not a photo. Export as flat PNG, no
transparency, exactly 1024x500px.
```

---

## Notes

- Generate both, pick whichever reads better at small size (test by
  shrinking the export to 200px wide on screen — if the wordmark blurs into
  the background, increase contrast).
- Keep the runner icon consistent with `src/assets/icon.png` — same
  gradient direction (blue leading edge → orange → green trailing) so it
  reads as the same brand mark across icon, splash screen, and this banner.
