## Notes for whoever fills in Play Console

- Keep "AI" in the short description and title — it's a genuine
  differentiator parents search for, and it's accurate (the coach really is
  AI-generated), so this isn't misleading.
- Do not claim things the app doesn't do yet (e.g. don't mention
  leaderboards or coach/parent dashboards in this copy until those are
  actually shipped — Play Console review will flag a mismatch between
  listing claims and app functionality).
- If/when Premium pricing is finalised, consider adding the exact price to
  the last paragraph (e.g. "starting at £4.99/month") — concrete pricing in
  the description tends to reduce paywall-related 1-star reviews from users
  who feel surprised by it.
